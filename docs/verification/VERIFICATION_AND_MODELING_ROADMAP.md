# Verification and Modeling Roadmap

IX-GCR-SPE is not allowed to jump from architecture sketch to performance
confidence without an explicit evidence ladder.

## Stage 0 — Claim hygiene
- finish source traceability,
- separate inference from evidence,
- identify all speculative branches.

## Stage 1 — Zone accounting
- validate zone and areal-density bookkeeping,
- check weak-zone continuity,
- confirm Shadow Vault geometry assumptions.

## Stage 2 — OLTARIS screening
- compare baseline passive vault vs uniform wall,
- compare water-rich, PE/UHMWPE, hydride-enhanced, and cryogenic hydrogen branches,
- evaluate SPE and GCR separately.

## Stage 3 — Geant4 geometry studies
- build simplified but explicit geometry,
- score mixed-field behavior,
- inspect secondaries and directional effects,
- compare hatch-path variants.

## Stage 4 — Shield-state degradation studies
- partial chamber depletion,
- leak-created void,
- hatch-path thinning,
- active-field unavailable scenarios.

## Stage 5 — Operational studies
- alerting timeline,
- crew motion into Shadow Vault,
- vault occupancy assumptions,
- event-duration sensitivity.

## Stage 6 — Future physical tests
- non-radiation mockups for fill-state and leak monitoring,
- structural and thermal prototype work for retained-mass systems,
- instrumentation prototype work for dosimetry and state estimation.

## Deliverable rule
Each stage should produce:
- assumptions,
- geometry,
- material list,
- outputs,
- uncertainties,
- pass/fail summary,
- decision on what to keep or kill.
